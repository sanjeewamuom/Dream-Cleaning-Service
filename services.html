Cleaning services in Mackay

Regular, deep, and end-of-lease home cleaning from $45 AUD per hour.

Regular home cleaning

Our standard clean keeps your home fresh on a weekly, fortnightly, or one-off basis. We dust and wipe surfaces, clean kitchen benches and splashbacks, sink and cooktop, then move through bathrooms (shower, vanity, toilet, mirrors) and finish with vacuum and mop of all floors.

Ideal for ongoing maintenance. We use a dedicated cloth system so kitchen and bathroom areas stay separate and hygienic. Add-ons like inside microwave, fridge exterior, or rangehood degrease can be quoted separately.

Deep cleaning

When you need more than a regular tidy, our deep clean adds skirting boards, fans, blinds, grout detailing, and mould spot treatment in bathrooms. We pay extra attention to behind the toilet, shower screen edges, and vent dust—areas that get missed in a quick wipe-down.

Perfect after a busy period, before guests, or when moving in. We work high-to-low and change water and cloths so every surface is properly cleaned. Oven and window-track cleaning can be included and quoted as add-ons.

End-of-lease & Airbnb

Bond and inspection-ready cleans follow a higher standard: inside cupboards (if empty), window tracks and sills, oven and rangehood where agreed, and thorough attention to bathrooms and kitchens. We focus on the areas property managers and landlords typically check.

We quote end-of-lease and Airbnb reset jobs individually based on property size, number of bathrooms, and scope (e.g. oven, inside fridge). Send us your address, bedroom and bathroom count, and move-out or turnover date for a free quote.
