About Dream Cleaning

A Mackay-based home cleaning service built on checklists, consistency, and care.

Why choose Dream Cleaning?

We follow a clear, repeatable system on every job: work high-to-low and clean-to-dirty, so dust is removed before wiping and mopping. Room-by-room checklists and a colour-coded cloth system keep kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas consistently clean and free from cross-contamination.

Mackay’s humidity, red dust, and coastal salt air need a cleaner who knows local conditions. We plan extra attention for mould-prone areas, entry mats, skirting boards, and windows, so your home stays fresh through wet season and beyond. We arrive on time, confirm the scope before we start, and leave your home safe and locked as agreed.

We want to be your dream service—reliable, thorough, and transparent. Book a regular clean, a deep clean, or an end-of-lease (bond) clean with confidence.
